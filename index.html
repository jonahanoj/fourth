    The Evergreen

    By Jonah Gann

    It was the year 2056. There was a spy organization that had just finished being built. It was time to hire some people. There were two kids, named David Kenler and Joshua Hackleburger. They were best friends. David and Joshua (whose nickname was Josh) always dreamed of being spies. They played games of being sneaky where they snuck into places that were evil and tried to break in and take back valuables from them. They would talk on their walkie talkies, saying things like “Hack into the control panel and turn off the laser barrier so I can get in.”

    Twenty-four years later (year 2080), David and Josh needed jobs. Remember that spy organization? Well, they were hiring again. The spy organization was called The Evergreen David saw a poster that read “The Evergreen spy organization (NOW HIRING!)” He asked Josh to come see it. They remembered that they wanted to be spies when they were kids. So they applied for the job!

    They had to take a test, sometime during the week, they had to sneak into one of the staff's houses and take something while there home. David did the test and succeeded! Josh on the other hand, did not. He failed and was caught. So, he tried again and failed again and again. On the twenty-seventh try, he finally did it! He was hired with David and a girl named Jacky. David, Josh and Jacky were Team B. One month later,they had their first mission, where they had to sneak into an evil headquarters called “The Darkeners,” to take back a powerful laser. When they got there, something seemed wrong because no one was there. They looked around and saw someone talking to a skinny guy. They listened in with their spy gear. They got the info they needed and told their spy leader, Mrs. Beth. They told her that it was in a place called “The Rock”. She told them that The Rock was a bar that sold soda, but no one liked their soda. So the place was abandoned and never to be heard of. She told them the location and they were off! When they got there, they were confused. They were in a forest and saw no bar. There were vines everywhere and everything was overgrown. Then Josh found something behind the vines. There was some wood! They went in even deeper and found a door! Or what used to be a door…

    They went in. There was a gray metal suitcase. They pulled up the latches, but it didn’t open. Then they saw a keyhole! They grabbed the suitcase and brought it back to HQ. When they got back, they got a drill and made a hole in the suitcase. They could see the laser! They brought it back to Mrs. Beth and she turned the laser on.

    “What are you doing!?” screamed David. It burnt a hole in the lock, and then she opened it. “I know what I’m doing,” Mrs. Beth said. She took the laser out and told them to bring it to the vault and then come back. So they did. When they came back, Mrs. Beth was gone! They then saw a hole in the window! They looked through it and saw someone carrying Mrs. Beth! She was all tied up, so they got the laser out of the vault and snuck up behind him. Jacky found a smoke bomb and threw it at the man. The man could not see, so they grabbed Mrs. Beth and David burned the rope with the laser. Josh asked, “Who was that!?” “It was one of the Darkener agents,” she said. So they then went into lockdown. The Darkeners were HERE. They searched the base to see if any broke in. They saw someone go into the vault and stole gems and gold! They saw him on camera, but he got away. So they gave up for a while and looked for clues. Later one of the Team c members found all of their valuables being sold on eBay. He showed Mrs. Beth immediately and she was shocked. So they scheduled a meet-up to buy the stolen items but they had to have a disguise. So Josh dressed up and got a new name, Henry. When they went up to him, he showed them the valuables, then “Henry” just took it and ran. The man who was selling the items chased Josh and then took him to their dungeon. There were high-tech prison doors and he was locked in. However, he had back-up. He got out his walkie-talkie and told David where he was, so they could sneak in. They were able to get Josh out using the laser, but when they were cutting the metal door, a guard came out! Luckily, they hid so he couldn't see them. Then the guard walked away, and they got him out. After that, they went back to base, and got all of the teams (Team, A, B, C, D, E, F and G.) Then, they asked a different spy organization called “The Pine” to help them. They all got ready to raid The Darkeners to put them in jail. The teams all went and put them in jail. They used the laser to open their vault and they retrieved the valuables!

    The Lighthouse

    By Jonah Gann

    Isaac and Chloe were siblings in a family of four. They lived in Maryland in a cabin in the forest by the ocean. There was an abandoned lighthouse by the shore. Its bulb was broken, its windows were shattered, and worst of all some people said that… it was haunted! Their parents said to never go inside. It was super broken and it could collapse at any minute! Some say that a wolf lives there but no one knows for sure. Isaac really wanted to go to the lighthouse but the rest of the family said no. So that night, Isaac was planning to sneak out. Night came and it was Isaac’s bed time. His parents tucked him in and said goodnight. Then, Isaac came out of bed and opened his window and snuck out. But, what he didn’t think of is that his sister frequently had nightmares and walks to his room and she did. Chloe wondered where he was and then she saw that the window was open. She was confused. Then, she looked out the window and saw the lighthouse’s door was open! She went to the front door and opened it up and went to the lighthouse, worried for her brother.

    Isaac went to see the bulb at the top floor. Isaac was quite the engineer and he was 14 years old.

    His curiosity as an engineer led him to try and fix the bulb. A while later, he had almost fixed it when his sister found him. “Why did you sneak out?!” said Chloe. “And why are you at the lighthouse? Mom and dad said to never go here!”

    “Done!” said Isaac and the bulb turned on. “Woah!” said Chloe, amazed. “Please don’t tell Mom and Dad,” said Isaac. “I wanna make this place look good.” “Fine,” said Chloe and they went home.

    The next day Chloe and Isaac went to the store and bought windows. Then, when night came they went to the lighthouse again. They put up the windows in the lighthouse but then they heard a wolf howl. They were terrified and they saw a shadow of a wolf. They thought it was the end. Then they saw the wolf. But it was just a baby! They named it Max and kept it as a pet in the lighthouse. But the next day their parents went stargazing and saw the lighthouse bulb was on! They went into the lighthouse and went to the top. They saw Isaac and Chloe and Max and they were mad. Chloe and Isaac were grounded for 3 weeks. But the family kept on building the lighthouse and they kept Max. A while later when the lighthouse was almost done Max was sniffing the floor looking for food. Then he found a secret trapdoor. Max started barking at it so the family went over. Chloe opened up the trapdoor and inside there was a bunch of sciency stuff. “What is all this?!” said isaac. “I don’t know,” said his mom. “It looks like a lab of some sort.” “Should we leave it here and ignore it? Or should we clean it up and make it like the rest of the lighthouse?” Said the dad. “I think we should leave it,” said Chloe. “It looks like someone has been here recently.” “How do you know?” said Isaac. “Look at this coffee mug, it still has coffee in it and it's still warm,” said Chloe. “What a detective!” said Dad.

    “Shhhh!” said the mom “Someone is coming!” They all hid under a table. A guy in a white lab coat came in and sat down at the table that they were under and picked up his coffee and took a slurp. Everyone was shaking terrified that he would see them. Then he left. They wanted to see what he was up to, so they started setting up a security camera. But, when they were almost done, he came back. “Who are you?” he said “Oh! You must be my test subjects.” Mom said, “Oh, ummm... we’re not your test subjects. We live here.” “No, I live here,” said the scientist. “You must be the people who made my lighthouse look better!” “Yes we are,” said Dad “How about we have a deal,” said the scientist. “You can live here since you made it look great. But, you have to let me live in the lab, ok?” “Deal!” said Mom and Dad.

    One day, the scientist made a portal to another dimension. He called them down to the lab, and everyone said the portal looked cool then they all went in. When they came through it was dark and hot. For some reason it was also slippery. They were standing on ice! But then why was it hot? Then they saw a light in a cave so they came in. Why was it light in a cave but dark outside? Then, they realized. They were in a parallel dimension! It's light in dark places and dark in light places and hot in cold places and probably cold in hot places! Then they went back through the portal as it was closing up. It probably wasn't safe there anyway. So they lived with the scientist and he made great coffee, but something was off about him. He acted strange. Yes, some scientists are strange, but this guy was crazy. He was like… a MAD scientist. He got squirrels from the woods and turned them into small hippos! Why? I do not know, but he used them as slaves. The family did not approve of that, so they released the squippos (squirrel hippos) one by one, under his nose. Until one day, he caught them releasing one. “WHAT ARE YOU DOING!?” he said. “ARE YOU AGAINST ME?! I'M TRYING TO GET AN ARMY OF SQUIPPOS!” “We are releasing them because you are using them as slaves!” said Chloe angrily. “I’m calling 911!” “NO!” said the scientist. He tried to whack the phone out of her hand. But he missed, and face planted into the floor! He fell unconscious. A moment later he woke up but they didn’t want him getting away. Then Max started barking and growling at him and pinned him down. A while later the police came and brought him to jail. After that, they started making the lab look like the rest of the lighthouse. They were going to sell the lab stuff on ebay, but didn’t want it getting into the wrong hands so they kept it hidden in a compartment in the lab. They finished the lighthouse a month later. They got famous. They got their own book about their story and that is the book you just finished.

      • Albert Einstein Research Paper

      By Jonah Gann

      Why was Albert Famous?

      The General Theory Of Relativity is what made Albert famous. Albert was famous because he was really good at science. He thought and answered questions he asked himself using his mind. How'd he get so smart? Well, he studied and studied. When he was an adult he sent his ideas to the magazines. The scientists were interested in him, so he worked with them. He thought that the world was made of tiny balls called atoms; they were so small you couldn't even see them. It is also how we know gravity today. He also made the equation E = mc2 , which means energy = mass times the speed of light squared. This is considered one of the most famous equations today. No one knows if he worked with his wife.

      The Story of Albert’s Life

      When Albert was three he started talking. His parents were worried about his behavior because he was different. Albert began to wonder about some of the mysteries of the world when his dad gave him a compass. Albert asked many questions about things we could not see. He was not like the other students in school. One day, Albert was riding his bike and looked at the beams of light and wondered what it would be like to ride on one. It was the biggest thought he ever had! It filled his mind with questions. Albert began to read and study about magnetism, gravity, light and sound. He read about numbers and thought they were like a secret language for figuring out things.

      So he kept on reading and learning and wondering. Albert wanted to be a teacher to teach his ideas, but he couldn't find any jobs so he worked in a government office. He finished his work quickly so he had extra time to wonder. When he watched a piece of sugar dissolve into his tea and watched smoke disappear into the air, he wondered what was really happening. He began to figure it out. Everything in the world is made of little bits of stuff called atoms! Then, Albert thought about motion; he realized that everything is always moving! He sent his ideas to the science magazines. The scientists were impressed! They asked Albert to work with them. People started to see that Albert was really smart! Albert wondered about big things and small things like the size of the universe or what happens inside atoms. He figured out that if he went the speed of light it would feel like minutes even though it had been years.

      What Helped Albert Make His Discoveries and Why are Albert’s Ideas Important?

      He studied a lot and thought a lot about the world. His wandering mind helped him uncover secrets of the world and studying helped him too. His ideas are important because they are real, like atoms and the general theory of relativity. His ideas went on to help later scientists create things based off of his discoveries, like GPS and electromagnetics. They have helped people understand how things move on the atomic level.

      Plants That Eat Living Things Research Paper

      By Jonah Gann

      Most plants eat water and nutrients from soil. However carnivorous plants eat living things like flies and bugs but they also have to be watered. They are most commonly found in acidic bogs. These plants have different trapping mechanisms. A few of these are listed below.

      1. Pitfall traps - Pitfall traps use pollen to attract their prey and waxy flakes which are slippery for insects, so they slip into a cup shaped area and the digestive enzymes break down the prey.
      2. Flypaper traps - Flypaper traps use a sticky glue like substance that the insects stick to.
      3. Snap traps - Snap traps are traps that make the leaves of the plant (sort of like a mouth) close on the insect. It detects the insect using little sensing hairs that make the leaves shut.
      4. Bladder traps - Bladder traps are bladder shaped pods on a plant they have a small opening that can open and close if a bug triggers sensing hairs that opens the hole in the bladder pod. There is water inside the pod and it creates a vacuum that sucks the bug in.
      5. Combination traps - Combination traps are a combination of 2 different types of traps.

      Souce: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Carnivorous_plant

      My Top 10 Picks for National Parks

      1. Yellowstone National Park

      Yellowstone is in two different states. It's mostly in Wyoming and a bit of it is in Montana. It has super cool Geyser holes that are RAINBOW. The geysers look cool in winter and you can chill and it’s peaceful. It also has a volcano under it.

      2. Great Smoky Mountains National Park

      Great Smoky Mountains National Park is in North Carolina and Tennessee. It looks super colorful in fall with all the colorful leaves. It looks beautiful in spring with tons of awesome flowers. It's a great place for hiking. It has 850 miles of trails. It’s also free to go to.

      3. Bryce Canyon National Park, Utah

      I am interested in this park because it has pillars of rock that look great for climbing. It also looks like you could turn it into a maze. Not only that, it has beautiful clear skies.

      4. Zion National Park, Utah

      Zion is one and a half hours from Bryce Canyon. It is like the Grand Canyon but more vegetation and it's smaller. It's a great place to go for dining at Springdale.

      5. Olympic National Park, Washington

      It is so beautiful and it is full of greenery. I would love to hike there. It also has awesome foggy oceans with caves.

      6. Acadia National Park, Maine

      There are so many colors! It's like a rocky beach by the ocean! It looks perfect for swimming! It also has huge mountains that go past the clouds. It's also about 100 years old!

      7. Glacier National Park, Montana

      There is some wildlife that goes great with the rocky mountain. There are some cute goats! And the little waterfalls look beautiful!

      8. Haleakala National Park, Hawaii

      It’s like a desert but also a mountain. And you are above the clouds! There are also beautiful sunsets!

      9. Denali National Park, Alaska

      It has a great view of a cool mountain. It looks straight out of a painting! There are also 6 million acres to roam! There are sometimes beautiful Auroras.

      10. Arches National Park, Utah

      It has awesome arches just like in the name. It is cool how the arches connect at the ends. It’s great for everyone! There are amazing different shapes of arches.
